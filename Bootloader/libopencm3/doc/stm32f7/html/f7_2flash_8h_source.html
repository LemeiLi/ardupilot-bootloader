42 /*
43  * Differences between F7 and F4:
44  * 1. icache and dcache are now combined into a unified ART cache. The CPU has
45  * its own d/i-caches, but those are unrelated to this. They are on the
46  * AXIM bus.
47  * 2. There's an OPTCR1 is now used for boot addresses. Write protect bits
48  * are in OPTCR. Why does F4 have 2 copies of nWRP?
49  * 3. Latency field in FLASH_ACR is now 4 bits. Some CPU frequencies supported
50  * by F7 require more than 7 wait states.
51  * 4. FLASH_SR_PGSERR (programming sequence error) is now FLASH_SR_ERSERR (
52  * erase sequence error).
53  * 5. FLASH_SR_BSY field is now read-only. Seems to also be read-only in F4?
54  * Why did we have a clear busy flag function?
55  * 6. There are now two watchdogs - IWDG (independent watchdog) and WWDG (
56  * window watchdog).
57  */
